Flight Predictions

CUSF Flight Predictor

Instructions

1- Set Lauch Altitude to 1450m (Rexburgs height above sea level)
2- Set your lauch time, typical lauch time is between 8AM - 9AM you will need to translate your lauch time
     to UTC. 8AM - 9AM is 15:00 - 16:00 UTC
3- Set your decent rate to 7 m/s
4- Click "Set With Map" then click the spot where you want to launch from
5- You can click hourly in the prediction type to give potental landing spots for the few hours
